Calico Critters

   

What are they?

A cute, collectible, and adorable series of baby figures with forest-themed accesories and costumes for hours of fun. They are not just dollhouse toys, they are a part of a much larger collection that includes playsets, miniature furniture, and even toy vehicles.

They come in different families, such as:

  1. Rabbits
  2. Cats
  3. Dogs
  4. Bears
  5. Pandas
  6. Racoons
  7. Porcupines
